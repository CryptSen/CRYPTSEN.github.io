About

CryptSen is a cryptocurrency analytics research platform, developed under the leadership of the AYCH inc.

CryptSen is provided software-as-a-service (SaaS), under the terms of the MIT license. It can be downloaded free of charge for self-hosting from our GitHub repository.

The AYCH inc offers an access to a hosted version of the software at demo.cryptsen.github.io. Please refer to our Terms and Conditions of Use for information.
